Spongy Dams and Superintelligence: The Unknowable Consequences of Engineering New Technologies

Massey College JFLS

October 2022
Lots of sustainability research can be connected by a single theme: unintended consequences. Engineering, both at small and large scales, has yet to develop ways of controlling for the unforseen risks of implementing new technology. Even more concerning is the fact that the consequences of these risks seem to be scaling with technological innovation. In this talk, I explore this potentially existential problem, and suggest heuristics that can help mitigate the error in 'trial and error'.
