Hello!

I am a graduate student at University of Toronto focused on aligning human development and technology with natural systems. My current research uses tools from industrial ecology, data science, and computational modelling to find ways of providing more housing with less embodied greenhouse gas emissions.

I am currently a Junior Fellow at Massey College and sit on the board of CCRI – Canada’s oldest housing co-op. I enjoy sharing my work online and in lectures.

When I am not cooped up in my lab, I fill my time with literature and athletics. I am an experienced rower and coach, and I have a fondness for readings on philosphy, technology, and antiquity.
